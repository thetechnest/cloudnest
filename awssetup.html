AWS Free Tier Account Setup Guide

This guide helps you create your AWS Free Tier account to begin your cloud journey.

Important Notes for Students

Why Every Student Should Have an AWS Free Tier Account

  • Your AWS Free Tier account is your personal cloud practice lab for real-world engineering skills.
  • AWS offers 12 months of Free Tier access to core services like EC2, S3, Lambda, and RDS.
  • Hands-on AWS experience helps you understand how modern industry infrastructure actually works.
  • AWS is the most widely used cloud platform globally, giving you strong placement relevance.
  • Many AI, ML, Data, and Software roles demand practical AWS exposure.
  • You can build mini-projects for your resume, GitHub, and technical interviews.
  • Early AWS learning helps you become future-ready and cloud-native.
  • AWS Free Tier allows safe learning with usage limits when best practices are followed.
  • Cloud + AI/ML + AWS together gives you a major career advantage.
  • Start now‚Äînot after graduation. AWS Free Tier is the smartest entry into cloud computing.

Step 1: Visit AWS Website

Visit https://aws.amazon.com/console/ and click Create a Free Account.

Step 2: Enter Login Details

Enter your email, password, and AWS account name.

Step 3: Account Type & Contact Info

Choose account type, enter personal details, and accept the agreement.

Step 4: Payment Verification

Enter card details for identity verification.
AWS places a small temporary verification charge which is refunded automatically.
