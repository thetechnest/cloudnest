Cloud Foundation introduces the core principles of cloud computing‚Äîscalability, elasticity, availability, resilience, and cost efficiency‚Äîthat explain why the cloud works the way it does. These concepts form the base for understanding every AWS service you will learn next.

Scalability

Ability to handle increasing users, data, or traffic smoothly.

Elasticity

Automatically scale resources up and down based on demand.

Availability

Ensures systems remain accessible with minimal downtime.

High Availability (HA)

Redundancy across zones or regions to avoid failures.

Agility

Rapid development and deployment without long delays.

Resilience

Ability to recover quickly from failures.

Fault Tolerance

Continues operating even when components fail.

Cost Efficiency

Pay only for what you use instead of upfront investment.

Global Reach

Cloud platforms provide worldwide regions and edge locations to deliver applications closer to users with low latency and high availability.
