Identity and Access Management (IAM) is a framework of policies, technologies, and controls that ensure the right users and services have appropriate access to cloud resources.

Simple Meaning: IAM controls login and permissions in the cloud.
  • Authenticates users and services
  • Authorizes actions on resources
  • Enforces security policies
üìò Exam Line: IAM defines who can access cloud resources and what actions they can perform.

3Ô∏è‚É£ Principle of Least Privilege

The principle of least privilege means granting only the minimum permissions required to perform a task ‚Äî nothing more.

Analogy: Giving a house key instead of the entire keychain.
  • Reduces security risks
  • Prevents accidental damage
  • Limits impact of compromised accounts
üìò Least privilege is a core IAM security principle.
