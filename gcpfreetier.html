GCP Free Tier Account Setup Guide

This guide helps you create your Google Cloud Free Tier account to begin your cloud journey.

Important Notes for Students

Why Every Student Should Have a GCP Free Tier Account

  • Your GCP Free Tier account is your personal cloud laboratory for real-world practice.
  • You receive free credits to explore Compute Engine, Cloud Storage, BigQuery, and Vertex AI.
  • Hands-on learning helps you understand how industry-grade systems actually work.
  • Practical GCP experience makes you internship, certification, and placement ready.
  • Many AI, ML, and Data roles today demand real cloud platform exposure.
  • You can build mini-projects for your resume, GitHub, and interviews.
  • Early cloud learning makes you future-ready and technology-adaptive.
  • Free Tier usage limits help you practice safely without billing risk.
  • Cloud + AI/ML skills together create a powerful career advantage.
  • Start now‚Äînot after graduation. GCP Free Tier is the safest entry into cloud.

Step 1: Visit Google Cloud Website

Visit https://cloud.google.com/ and click Get Started for Free.

Step 2: Sign in with Google Account

Sign in using your existing Google account or create a new one.

Step 3: Enter Account & Billing Details

Enter your country, accept the terms, and provide card details for identity verification.

Step 4: Free Credits Activation

Google Cloud activates free credits which you can use for your practice and projects.
Google Cloud also performs a small temporary verification charge which is refunded automatically.
