Learn Cloud Skills — From Fundamentals to Real-World Deployments

CloudNest introduces learners to how cloud computing powers modern Software Engineering Applications, DataScience, AI/ML workflows/workloads—from scalable training clusters to real-time inference pipelines—and opens doors to emerging domains like Quantum Computing, where cloud providers offer simulators and quantum development kits. Understanding cloud architecture is becoming essential in the era of AI/ML, as organizations rely on cloud-native infrastructure to build intelligent applications at global scale.

At NBKRIST, we believe that true engineering education is built through hands-on experience and real-world exposure. Our advanced cloud laboratories empower students with practical skills on live industry platforms. We bridge the gap between classroom learning and professional IT practice through real-time projects and lab-driven training. By equipping students with future-proof cloud skills, we prepare them for global careers in the IT industry. NBKRIST proudly stands for democratizing Cloud Computing, ensuring that every student has access to cutting-edge technology. Our mission is to build confident, skilled, and industry-ready engineers.
