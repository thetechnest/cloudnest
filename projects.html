Foundation

The foundation of this project portfolio is rooted in the practical adoption of the AI/ML lifecycle using cloud computing, reflecting how modern intelligent systems are designed, deployed, and operated in real-world environments. The projects emphasize an end-to-end AI/ML pipeline architecture, covering every stage from data ingestion and preprocessing to model training, deployment, monitoring, and continuous improvement.

Special attention is given to the development of an AI/ML pipeline for healthcare applications, where reliability, scalability, and ethical handling of sensitive medical data are critical. By leveraging cloud-based AI for disease prediction, these projects demonstrate how healthcare systems can evolve from reactive treatment approaches to proactive and predictive care models.

Furthermore, the foundation incorporates medical AI architecture using cloud platforms and Large Language Models (LLMs), showcasing how advanced AI capabilities integrate with scalable cloud infrastructure to support intelligent diagnostics, clinical decision support, and patient-centric solutions. Overall, this initiative establishes a cloud-enabled AI/ML lifecycle for healthcare, enabling students to build robust, secure, and future-ready medical AI systems aligned with contemporary industry practices.
