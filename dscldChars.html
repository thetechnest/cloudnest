Characteristics of Cloud Computing

Introduction (Industry Perspective)

From my experience as a cloud consultant, understanding cloud characteristics is the foundation of cloud technology. These characteristics explain how cloud platforms deliver scalability, reliability, and cost efficiency. Once these fundamentals are clear, advanced services in AWS, Azure, and GCP become easier to understand and implement.

6. Which feature enables Multi-Tenancy?

Shared infrastructure with secure logical isolation, is called Multi-tenancy.
Multi-tenancy is enabled by virtualization, which allows multiple customers to share the same physical infrastructure while keeping their resources logically isolated. Each tenant operates as if they have a dedicated environment, even though hardware is shared.
