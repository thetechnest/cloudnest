AI / ML Lifecycle using Cloud (Healthcare Use Case)

Introduction

In large hospital research environments, AI/ML systems must process massive volumes of clinical data, train complex models, and deploy them securely. Cloud computing enables every stage of the AI/ML lifecycle with scalability, compliance, and cost efficiency.
